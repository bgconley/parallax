# Content Constraints and Copy Rules

## Titles
- 1 line preferred
- 2 lines acceptable only for long object names
- avoid jargon

## Subtitles
- orient the user
- 1 line preferred, 2 lines max
- should answer: why am I here or what will happen next?

## Helper AI card copy
- supportive, specific, plain
- should sound competent and warm
- should never sound overly emotional, childish, or like a chatbot persona

Good:
- I shortened your next block and moved two flexible tasks later.
- You don’t need to organize it perfectly right now.
- I used your context to make the steps fit this email better.

Bad:
- Don’t worry, bestie! We’ve got this 💖
- I analyzed your productivity graph and optimized your task vector.
- Great job!!!!!!!

## Chip labels
- 1–3 words preferred
- 4 words max in exceptional cases
- should map to an established semantic family

## Bottom actions
- Primary should be action-forward and concrete.
- Secondary should be a realistic alternate path.
- Tertiary should be lower commitment or navigational.
