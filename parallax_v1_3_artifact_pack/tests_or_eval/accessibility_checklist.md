# Accessibility Checklist

- Minimum touch target is at least 44 pt.
- Preferred touch target is at least 48 pt.
- Dynamic Type variants exist for primary screens.
- High contrast variants exist for primary screens.
- Color is not the only signal for temporal state.
- Timer and review screens remain usable with reduced motion.
- Evidence cards are readable by screen readers.
- Error and pending states are announced clearly.
